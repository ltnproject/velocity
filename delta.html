Installation Guide

Follow the steps for your platform below.

  1. 1
    Download — Click the Windows button above to download the BlueStacks installer (.exe).
  2. 2
    Install BlueStacks — Run the installer and follow the on-screen setup wizard.
  3. 3
    Sign in to Google — Open BlueStacks and sign into your Google account to access the Play Store.
  4. 4
    Install Roblox — Search for Roblox in the Play Store and install it inside BlueStacks.
  5. 5
    Download Delta APK — Also grab the Android APK above and drag it into the BlueStacks window to install.
  6. 6
    Inject & Execute — Open Roblox in BlueStacks, launch Delta, inject, and run your script.
Use a secondary Roblox account. Requires Windows 10/11 64-bit.
  1. 1
    Download APK — Tap the Android button above to download the APK file.
  2. 2
    Allow Unknown Sources — Go to Settings → Security → enable "Install unknown apps" for your browser.
  3. 3
    Install — Open the downloaded APK and tap Install.
  4. 4
    Open Delta — Launch the app and grant any requested permissions.
  5. 5
    Open Roblox — Launch Roblox mobile and join a game.
  6. 6
    Inject & Execute — Switch to Delta, tap Inject, then paste and run your script.
Requires Android 8.0+. Use a secondary Roblox account.

📺 Install Guide — eSign by applejr

  1. 1
    Watch the video — Follow applejr's eSign tutorial above to set up sideloading on your device.
  2. 2
    Download IPA — Tap the iOS button above to get the .ipa file.
  3. 3
    Install via eSign — Open eSign, import the IPA, and install Delta onto your device.
  4. 4
    Trust Developer — Go to Settings → General → VPN & Device Management → trust the Delta certificate.
  5. 5
    Open Roblox — Launch Roblox and join a game.
  6. 6
    Inject & Execute — Switch to Delta, inject, and run your script.
Requires iOS 14+. Sideloaded apps may need re-signing every 7 days on free Apple accounts.
